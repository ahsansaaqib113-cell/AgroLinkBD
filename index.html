Empowering Local Farmers Since 2026

Empowering Bangladeshi Farmers, Nourishing Communities

AgroLink BD was founded with a simple vision: to bridge the gap between hard-working rural farmers and urban consumers. Traditional agricultural supply chains in Bangladesh often leave farmers with minimal profits due to brokers, while consumers pay premium prices for stored goods.

Our platform enables direct farm-to-table delivery, ensuring transparency, maximum freshness, and higher profit margins for smallholder agricultural communities.
